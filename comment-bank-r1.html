Ownership of LearningMotivationBringing passions, interests, and voice to the classroomYou bring your passions, interests, and voice into the classroom.Find opportunities to connect your outside interests to what's happening in class. When an assignment gives you latitude, bring in your own perspective rather than defaulting to what seems to be expected.
Ownership of LearningMotivationLearning for learning's sakeYou prioritize the process of exploring ideas, rather than focusing on outcomes and grades.Resist the urge to focus solely on the grade.
